About Al Furqan Scholars Academy

Al Furqan Scholars Academy is dedicated to spreading authentic Islamic knowledge in English, Arabic & Urdu. Our courses include Dars-e-Nizami, Quran recitation & tafseer, Seerat-e-Rasool ﷺ, and short courses for children. Our teachers are experienced scholars trained in Shariah and Dars-e-Nizami methodology.
